# EILINAIRE FUEL: Master Framework 2026
## The Biological Flavor Wave

> Source: Prem (founder) | Stored by Jeff | 2026-05-28
> **Core Concept:** Functional food system designed as a "life manual" aligned with Circadian Rhythm. Divided into 4 axes that escalate flavor intensity and texture to precisely stimulate the user's physiology and psychology.

---

## 1. FOCUS (Breakfast 07:00)

**Designed for:** Gently wake the body, provide stable energy for concentration and mental sharpness to start the day.

- **Intensity:** 2/4 (gentle, umami, light)
- **Highlight:** Efficiency & Clarity (fast to eat, alert mind, sharp brain)
- **Character:** **"The Precise Bite" (Solid-Smooth)** — Stable, smooth, geometric forms. Easy to chew but not mushy. Minimizes visual and chewing processing so the brain can fully focus on work.
- **Biological Mechanism:** Stomach just woke up, not ready for heavy food. Gentle umami and complex carbs maintain stable blood sugar without Sugar Crash.

---

## 2. RESET (Lunch 13:00)

**Designed for:** Snap attention back and cure Brain Fog with light, refreshing flavors. Awakens senses without causing post-meal drowsiness.

- **Intensity:** 3/4 (fresh, sharp, airy, sour)
- **Highlight:** Instant Reboot (refreshing, revives body from fatigue)
- **Character:** **"The Clean Snap" (Crisp & Springy)** — Fresh vegetable crunch and springy noodle resistance. Stimulates the Trigeminal Nerve creating subtle vibrations that wake the body.
- **Biological Mechanism:** Afternoon cortisol dip. Sharp sour flavors and fresh herb aromas act like splashing cold water on the face — instant fatigue reset.

---

## 3. PRESENT (Dinner 18:00)

**Designed for:** A "reward" after the workday. Anchors awareness to the present moment with the deepest, most complex flavors.

- **Intensity:** 4/4 (rich sauce, round, heavy)
- **Highlight:** Sensory Reward (happiness reward, present-moment awareness)
- **Character:** **"The Full Indulgence" (Rich & Multi-Layered)** — Intense, multi-dimensional textures: soft, chewy, and explosive aromas (e.g., Wok Hei). Captures all attention on flavor.
- **Biological Mechanism:** Body craves replenishment after a full day of energy expenditure. Heavy flavors and complex aromas stimulate the brain's reward system, triggering maximum dopamine release.

---

## 4. CALM (Late Night 21:00)

**Designed for:** Shut down the body. Minimal seasoning to reduce digestive load. Transition physiology to rest mode.

- **Intensity:** 1/4 (plain, mild, natural)
- **Highlight:** Gentle Recovery (soothing, comforting the body)
- **Character:** **"The Velvet Cloud" (Silky & Airy)** — Cotton-like or silk texture, fine and smooth, light, melts in mouth with almost no chewing required. Signals the body it's time to rest.
- **Biological Mechanism:** Digestive system preparing for Safe Mode. Light textures and gentle therapeutic aromas help lower heart rate and prepare for deep sleep.
