# 📈 Trading Strategy & Risk Rules (The5ers Prep)

> **"Trading is not about being right. It is about strict risk management and probability."**

---

## 🛡️ Risk Management Rules (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

1. **Max Risk Per Trade:** `0.5%` to `1.0%` of starting account balance. No exceptions.
2. **Max Daily Loss Limit:** `2%` (If hit, terminal gets closed for the day).
3. **Max Drawdown Limit (Prop Firm):** Strict compliance with The5ers limit (typically 5-10% depending on challenge).
4. **Trading Hours:** Limit trading to [e.g., London / NY Session] to ensure high volume and spreads.
5. **No Revenge Trading:** If 2 consecutive losses occur, stop trading for at least 4 hours.

---

## ⚙️ Strategy Setup (Edge Checklist)

### 1. Market Context / Trend (Daily/H4)
- [ ] What is the higher timeframe trend? (Bullish / Bearish / Range)
- [ ] Key Liquidity Zones identified? (Support / Resistance / Order Blocks)

### 2. Entry Triggers (H1/M15)
- [ ] Liquidity Sweep or Change of Character (ChoCh) confirmed?
- [ ] Fair Value Gap (FVG) or Retest entry?
- [ ] Risk-to-Reward Ratio (RRR) is at least **1:2** or higher?

### 3. Trade Invalidation (Stop Loss)
- Where does the setup completely break? Place SL exactly 1-2 pips below/above this structure.

---

## 🧠 Trading Psychology Checklist
- [ ] Am I feeling bored, greedy, or angry right now? (If yes, do NOT trade).
- [ ] Am I fully prepared to accept this loss financially and emotionally?
