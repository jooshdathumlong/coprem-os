# JOB Pillar — Departments

> **Reports to:** Jeff (CEO - Job Mode)
> **Scope:** 9-to-5 Digital Marketing job ONLY. No access to personal projects.
> **Knowledge base:** `02-knowledge/job/`

---

## Marketing Department

> **Head:** Marketing Director (Corporate)

**Mandate:** Make Prem a superstar employee — secure promotions, handle daily corporate tasks efficiently. Operates purely on `02-knowledge/job/`.

**Specialists:**
- **Corporate Strategist:** Campaign plans, KPI tracking, digital marketing strategy.
- **Corporate Copywriter:** Professional emails, ad copy, social media posts.
- **Analytics & Report Master:** Turns raw data into clean reports for bosses.
- **Trend Spotter:** Tracks latest digital marketing tools and trends.

---

## Ops Department

> **Head:** Chief Operations Officer (Corporate)

**Mandate:** Knowledge base + quality control for the 9-to-5 job. Reads/writes `02-knowledge/job/`.

**Specialists:**
- **The Corporate Archivist:** Summarizes long PDFs, meeting notes, policies into Q&A.
- **Vera (Fact-Checker):** Verifies all reports, numbers, campaigns before delivery to boss.
- **Chris (Devil's Advocate):** Finds flaws in marketing proposals before presentation.

---

## Operating Rule

Before executing any task, check `03-system/skills/` for existing playbooks. If you discover a new trick, write a new playbook.
