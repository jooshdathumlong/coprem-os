# Business Projects Single Source of Truth (SSOT)

## 1. Real Estate: Sunset Apartment
**Core Concept:** A 4-story, low-to-medium risk residential apartment with a built-in cafe.
- **Budget:** 5M THB.
- **Target Audience:** Healthcare workers, government officials, and medical students.
- **Location:** 54.7 sq.wah at Pracha Chuen (Near Ministry of Public Health, hospitals).
- **Positioning:** Quiet, quality living for medical/government staff. High privacy, pet-friendly.
- **Economics:** Land cost 2.2M THB. Target rent: 4,200 - 5,200 THB/month (Studio & 1-Bedroom, 24-32 sqm). Expected occupancy: 75-85% (Year 1) to 90-95% (Stabilized).
- **Architecture:** 
  - 1st Floor: Owner's Residence & Parking (3 Cars, 8 Motorcycles).
  - 2nd Floor: Cafe, Lobby, Gym, Laundry.
  - 3rd-4th Floors: 6-8 Rental Units. No elevator.
- **Exit Strategy:** Long-term hold.

## 4. Retail & Fashion: ClothShop (Men's Casual)
**Core Concept:** Casual everyday men's wear with a "Royal/Elegant" vibe.
- **Target Audience:** Men seeking to upgrade their style and appearance.
- **Price Point:** 200 - 1,500 THB (Medium-High quality).
- **Market Sizing:** Estimated 930M THB market potential in Thailand based on demographic targeting.
- **Strategy:** Start with low capital, handmade/custom selections, utilizing color theory (Personal Color) to solve men's styling pain points.
