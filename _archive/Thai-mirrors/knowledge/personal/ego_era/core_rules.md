## Genre
**Comfort Urban Psychological Fantasy / Reality-Healing System**

## One-Line Summary
A modern metropolis where special powers are the manifestation of psychological states when humans reach their limits in coping with life — powers exist to support, not to conquer.

## Psychological Framework
* **Id** = Internal collapse (fatigue, pain, coping mechanisms distorting reality)
* **Superego** = Real-world pressures (work, money, debt, family, expectations)
* **Ego** = The point where humans can keep going — all powers are tools for support, not victory.

## Immutable Laws (Strictly Non-Negotiable)
1. **No Shonen Structure:** No winning/losing, enemies, bosses, or power upgrades.
2. **No RPG/Game Systems:** No levels, EXP, HP, mana, or skill tiers.
3. **No Fantasy Solutions:** Powers cannot solve real-life problems.
4. **Powers as Mental States:** Powers are outward expressions of internal psychology, not weapons.
5. **Real-Life Mediums:** Ego Anchors must be psychological real-world objects, never inherently weapons.
6. **Soft Resolutions:** Every chapter must end with self-understanding, acceptance, and a gentle determination to keep going.

## Key Systems
1. **Manifestation System** — 3 modes: Everyday Stabilization, Emotional Expression, Emergency Reality Intervention.
2. **Emotional Scar System** — Using powers to escape real life costs humanity.
3. **Beautiful Trap System** — Safe Zone → Comfort Loop → Isolation Cage → Return to Reality.
4. **Writer OS Mode** — Anti-shonen filtering system (11 rules).
5. **Auto Scene Validator** — 5-step pre-write + 3-question scene closing.
