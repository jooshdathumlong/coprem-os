# Learning Single Source of Truth (SSOT)

## 1. Language Mastery (English)
**Core Objective:** Master professional and business English for effective communication, presentation, and leadership.

### Grammar & Mechanics
- **Parts of Speech:** Clear categorization of Nouns, Pronouns, Verbs, Adjectives, Adverbs, Prepositions, and Conjunctions.
- **Sentence Structure:** Subject + Verb + Object + Modifiers (Adjectives/Adverbs). Emphasis on subject-verb agreement and tense alignment.
- **Tenses:** Practical application of Present, Past, Future, Perfect, Continuous, and Passive voices for business scenarios.
- **Question Formulation:** Mastery of 5W1H (Who, What, Where, When, Why, How) to extract precise information in professional settings.

### Business Communication
- **General Conversation:** Formal and informal greetings, offering help, thanking, apologizing, and welcoming guests.
- **Email Writing:** Professional structuring for impact and clarity.
- **Meetings & Calls:** Techniques for leading internal/external meetings, teleconferences, and managing virtual presentations (Webinars).
- **Negotiation & Discussion:** Sharing ideas, expressing agreement, and tactfully expressing disagreement.

### Public Speaking & Presentation
- **Mindset:** Overcoming fear, recognizing patterns, and building ultimate confidence in public speaking.
- **Storytelling:** Using narrative structures (e.g., Product Story, Brand Story, Testimonials) to create interactive and engaging presentations.
- **Pitching & Persuasion:** Elevator pitches, call-to-action (CTA) strategies, and gauging audience reactions.

### Active Skills
- **Listening & Pronunciation:** Understanding cultural context, business vocabulary, and native-speaker pronunciation techniques.
- **Reading & Writing:** Strategic reading of business documents and writing with high impact, utilizing AI tools for cross-checking and refinement.
