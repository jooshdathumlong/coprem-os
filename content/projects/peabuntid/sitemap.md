## 1. UX Overview

**Design Principle:** Easy to understand, minimal steps. Users reach what they need quickly without complex processes.

**Main Navigation:**
1. **Shop:** Curated products (by category)
2. **Knowledge Base:** Verified, summarized content
3. **Community Explorer:** Geographic search of curated local data
4. **Tell the Scholar:** Submit data for review
5. **Help & Support:** Guides and team contact

**Global Elements:** Search, Cart
