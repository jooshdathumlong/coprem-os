# Personal (Me) Single Source of Truth (SSOT)

## 1. Core Life Philosophy
- **Memento Mori:** Constantly remember our mortality. Life is short; do not wait until it is too late to realize what truly matters. (Taoist influences).
- **Personal Development:** Focus on health, stylistic alignment (e.g., Soft Warm Autumn personal color), and continuous self-improvement.

## 2. Professional Prioritization (Marketing & Sales Frameworks)
**Marketing Strategy:**
- **AIDA Model Execution:**
  - *Attention:* Content marketing, SEO, Ads, PR to create brand awareness.
  - *Interest:* Deep-dive content, webinars, case studies to build trust.
  - *Desire:* Testimonials, demos, free trials to create product demand.
  - *Action:* Clear CTAs, promotions, and seamless checkout processes to drive conversions.
- **Retention:** Follow-ups, loyalty programs, and continuous communication to build brand advocates.
- **Analysis & Planning:** PESTEL (Macro), Direct/Indirect Competitor analysis, SWOT (Internal), SMART goals, Customer Personas, and the 7Ps of Marketing.

**Sales Strategy:**
- **Structure:** Defined roles, responsibilities, and clear Sales Team Structure.
- **Process:** The 7 Stages of the Sales Process, documented in a formal Sales Playbook.
- **Metrics:** Quantitative and qualitative objectives, KPIs, and dedicated Sales Budgets.

**Smarketing (Sales + Marketing Alignment):**
- Regular Smarketing Meetings.
- Closed-Loop Feedback systems.
- Defined Service Level Agreements (SLAs) between departments.
